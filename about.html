About

WHO ARE WE?

We are Jeff Thompson, Mark Ableidinger, and Elizabeth Rives. On the street we go by the name "Team Edward's Pop and Lock Crunk Crew."

WHAT WE DID

First and foremost, we wanted to make something fun. After bouncing ideas off of each other for a while, we decided that we wanted to make a choose-your-own-adventure style game. We did some research and found that there are a lot of these types of games around online. However, they tend to be difficult and cumbersome to navigate, and they were also very ugly websites. We decided to make our own version with an emphasis on being visually interesting and very easy to read and use.
